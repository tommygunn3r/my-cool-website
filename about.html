About Us

The Mastermind (or Mad Scientist?)

Gunner's Games is run by none other than Tommygunner, a person who discovered that asking AI to make games is somehow more entertaining than actually playing them. Who knew?

"Making stupid games with AI is more fun than it should be."

Our Origin Story

Founded in 2025, Gunner's Games was born from a simple realization: if you're going to waste time, you might as well waste it creatively. What started as "let's see what happens if I ask an AI to make a game" quickly spiraled into a full-blown collection of digital shenanigans.

What We Do

We harness the power of artificial intelligence to create games that range from "surprisingly decent" to "what were we thinking?" Each game is a unique blend of human creativity and AI chaos, resulting in entertainment that's 100% authentic and 0% guaranteed to make sense.

Our Philosophy

Life's too short for boring games. Whether you're spinning slots, flipping cards, or blasting aliens (probably), we believe gaming should be fun, ridiculous, and occasionally baffling. If you're not laughing at our games, you're at least laughing at the fact that they exist.

So grab a controller (or keyboard, or touchscreen, or whatever), pick a game, and prepare for an experience that's been lovingly crafted by a human-AI dream team that may or may not know what they're doing.

Welcome to Gunner's Games. It's weird here. We like it that way.
